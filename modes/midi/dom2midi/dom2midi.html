1. Drag dom2midi to your bookmarks.
  2. On any page, click on the bookmark to generate a MIDI file from the structure of the page.
  3. You can then also change and save the settings which determine the relationship of page structure to instruments, pitches, and rhythms.
(I believe that this only works in Firefox: IE doesn't handle either bookmarklets or data URIs well; Chrome just has a problem with midi data in data URIs.) (It's also got some out-right bugs on longer, more complex pages: the generated MIDI is corrupt in some way.)
